MS Fleet Service - Transparent Interactive Outlook Signature

How to install:
1. Unzip this folder.
2. Open MS_Fleet_Interactive_Outlook_Signature.html in Chrome or Edge.
3. Press Ctrl+A / Cmd+A, then Ctrl+C / Cmd+C.
4. Open Outlook > Settings > Signatures.
5. Paste it into the signature editor and save.

Important:
This version has the black background removed. It will blend better into the email body, but because the signature uses white text, it looks best on dark or neutral email backgrounds. On a pure white email background, some white text may be hard to see.
